How We Build

Manufacturing Process

Every Tracko product goes through a rigorous multi-stage manufacturing process to ensure OEM-grade quality.

01

Alloy Casting

Premium aluminum and iron alloys are melted and gravity-cast or centrifugally cast into near-net shapes.

02

CNC Machining

Multi-axis CNC centers machine critical dimensions to ±0.005mm tolerances with automated inspection.

03

Heat Treatment

T6 heat treatment and age-hardening processes optimize material strength and thermal stability.

04

Quality Inspection

100% dimensional inspection with CMM, hardness testing, and surface finish verification before shipping.
