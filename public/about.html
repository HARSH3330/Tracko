Our Story

Engineering Excellence for 18+ Years

Founded in 2005, Tracko Parts began as a small machining shop in Rajkot, Gujarat — the heart of India's engineering district. With a vision to manufacture world-class engine components, we invested in precision CNC technology and built a team of experienced metallurgists and automotive engineers.

Today, Tracko is a trusted name in automotive aftermarket, supplying precision pistons, rings, liners, and engine components to distributors and engine rebuilders across 40+ countries. Our 50,000 sq ft facility houses state-of-the-art casting, machining, and quality inspection equipment.

Every Tracko product is manufactured under strict ISO 9001:2015 quality standards with 100% dimensional inspection using CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machines) before shipment.

Our Capabilities

Manufacturing Facilities

Our 50,000 sq ft facility is equipped with advanced manufacturing and testing equipment.

🏭 Casting Division

Gravity die casting and centrifugal casting with capacity of 5,000 units/day. Aluminum alloy and cast iron processing.

⚙ CNC Machining

Multi-axis CNC turning and milling centers with ±0.005mm tolerance capability. Automated tool changers for production efficiency.

🔬 Quality Lab

CMM inspection, hardness testing, surface profilometry, and metallographic analysis. 100% inspection on all critical dimensions.

🔥 Heat Treatment

T6 heat treatment, solution treatment, and age-hardening with programmable furnaces and precise temperature control.

🛡 Surface Coating

Graphite, DLC, ceramic, Moly, and chrome plating capabilities for anti-friction and thermal barrier coatings.

📦 Packaging

Individual VCI packaging with custom branding options. Anti-corrosion wrapping for overseas shipment. OEM labeling available.
